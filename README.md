# World-Population-Analysis
Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA) on 234 countries uncovering population shifts from 1970 to 2022,
Engineered features (Population Momentum), 
detected India/China data discrepancy,
Time-Series Analysis;I calculated growth over time also i calculated the percentage change between 1970 to 2022 
to see long-term trends
delivered policy insights.  
## Key Insight: 
- China+India = 35% world population.
- Top 3 countries with most population is China,India and USA 
- Africa growing 4x faster than Europe.  
- Population decline in European countries
- High Growth countries are  in Africa
- The most populous continent is Asia
- The countries with shrinking population are Ukraine,Italy,Japan and Bulgaria
## Tools: 
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Jupyter Notebook.
